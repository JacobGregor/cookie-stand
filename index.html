About Salmon Cookies!

Salmon Cookies is a PNW based company out of Seattle Washington, dedicated to our high quality design and all natural ingredients. Our designs are inspired by the beautiful King, Chinook and CoHo salmon that roam that call the Oceans and Rivers on Washington home. Come into any of our locations and ask us about how you can get involved in protecting and conserving the species and ecosystems.
